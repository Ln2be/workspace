Trip 1

Day 1: Arrival at the airport directly to hotel. Dinner and night stay at the hotel.
Day 2: After breakfast, visit the capital market to exchange money and get a SIM card. Visit the camel market and return to the hotel for lunch. In the afternoon, visit the National Museum and the fish market. Attend a nomadic camp for milk training. Dinner and night stay at the hotel.
Day 3: After breakfast, head to the northern part of Mauritania to Terjit. Have lunch in Terjit and then head directly to Chinguetti. Witness the sunset and return to the hotel for dinner and night stay.
Day 4: After breakfast, explore Chinguetti mosque and library. Visit the town of Chinguetti and then head directly to Oudane. Have lunch at the hotel and explore the old city, mosque, and library. Witness the sunset and return to the hotel for the night stay.
Day 5: After breakfast, travel from Oudane to Atar. Explore the city of Atar, its market, and museum. After lunch, proceed to Choum and spend the night on the train.
Day 6: Arrival at Nouadhibou. Have breakfast and lunch at the hotel. In the afternoon, explore the Port of Fish and the town. Return to the hotel for dinner and night stay.
Day 7: After breakfast, head to Nouakchott directly to the hotel for the night stay.
Day 8: Return to the airport. Trip ends.

Trip 2

Day 1: Arrival in Nouakchott. Check-in at the hotel.
Day 2: Depart from Nouakchott to Parc National du Banc D’Arguin. Travel on the Iron train and camp along the route.
Day 3: Continue the journey on the Iron Train to Ben Amera and camp overnight.
Day 4: Travel from Ben Amera to Atar. Camp at Bab Sahara and have dinner.
Day 5: Explore the canyon and desert around Atar.
Day 6: Journey from the desert to Richad Structure and then to Ouadane.
Day 7: Visit Ouadane and proceed to Chinguetti.
Day 8: Explore Chinguetti, Zarga, Mehaïret, and Madden. Camp overnight.
Day 9: Camp at Ouade Hnouk and Malleh.
Day 10: Camp at Tidjekja and Mattmata.
Day 11: Return to Nouakchott and check-in at the hotel.
Day 12: Departure from Nouakchott.
